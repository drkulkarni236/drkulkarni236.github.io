Overwiew

WindSmart is an end-to-end predictive maintenance system designed for the offshore wind turbine industry. By offering early anomaly detection and predictive maintenance capabilities, WindSmart assists offshore wind engineers in optimizing operations and reducing downtime. This cost-effective solution is crucial in an industry with narrow profit margins, making renewable energy investments financially viable.

Key Features

  • Market Analysis: WindSmart's development began with a comprehensive market analysis, in collaboration with subject matter experts, to identify opportunities and assess potential impacts in the offshore wind turbine industry. This strategic approach ensured that the product is tailored to industry standards and requirements.
  • Anomaly Detection: WindSmart employs advanced data analytics, including Gaussian Mixture Models (GMM), to detect operational anomalies in wind turbines. It provides engineers with anomaly flags, top contributing variables, and real-time data insights for proactive decision-making.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Leveraging a comprehensive two-year dataset from four turbines, WindSmart provides actionable insights into turbine performance, enabling engineers to make informed decisions through intuitive data visualizations and reports.
  • User-Centric Design: The Flask-based web application offers an intuitive interface tailored to wind turbine engineers. It facilitates anomaly investigation, identifies contributing factors, and streamlines maintenance planning.
  • Cloud-Based Deployment: WindSmart is hosted on AWS, utilizing an EC2 instance for computation. This cloud-based approach ensures scalability, accessibility, and reliability for users.

Impact

WindSmart empowers offshore wind engineers with proactive maintenance tools, enhancing operational efficiency and ultimately bolstering profit margins in the renewable energy sector. By reducing downtime and maintenance costs, WindSmart makes renewable energy investments more financially viable, encouraging further growth in this essential industry and contributing to climate initiative targets.

Future Work

Future developments include refining data modeling with synthetic data to address failure imbalances and exploring federated learning models for privacy-conscious industries. Collaboration opportunities with Github or Hugging Face may further expand project availability and awareness.

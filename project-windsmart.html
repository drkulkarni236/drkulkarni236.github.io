Overwiew

In this project, my team and I built an end-to-end failure prediction system for offshore wind turbines. This included a comprehensive market analysis to identify opportunities and assess potential impact. To ensure the product's relevance and effectiveness, we engaged with subject matter experts who provided invaluable insights into the requisite features. The data was gathered from operational wind turbines at Energias de Portugal, a Portuguese electric utilities company. A Gaussian Mixture Model (GMM) was used to detect anomalies in the turbine operation. Our end product is a Flask-based web app hosted on AWS, tailored towards wind turbine engineers to facilitate predictive maintenance. The app flags the anomalous data points in the turbine operation and provids the top three variables contributing to the anomaly, allowing the users to further investigate the cause and, if necessary, trigger on-site maintenance evaluations.

Technologies: Python, AWS, Flask, Docker
